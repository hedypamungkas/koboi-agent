# Northwind Cloud -- Support FAQ

## What is your refund policy?

Enterprise and Pro tier customers get a 30-day money-back guarantee on
subscription fees. Free-tier usage is not refundable. Refunds are processed
within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

## What are your support SLAs?

Enterprise tier: 1-hour first response, 24/7. Pro tier: 4-hour first response,
business hours only. Free tier: best-effort, community forum only.

## How do I escalate to a human?

Say "talk to a human" or "speak to an agent" at any point and the assistant
will hand off the conversation with a summary of what's been discussed so far.
