Photoshop: color overlay. In my sunlight touch up tutorial, I showed you how to add a hint of color to your photo while giving it an extra boost at the same time. However, sometimes a photo can benefit from a little color, but just doesn’t need that extra boost. Or even a minor light tweak. This simple color overlay is a great way to add a touch of color without making your photo look like you’ve edited the heck out of it. Or to add a little life to B&W photos. Note: this works the exact same way in Elements as it does in Photoshop.
