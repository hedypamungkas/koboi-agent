2 doctors weighed in: Tooth extraction: how long does it take to fully heal? 2 doctors weighed in. Want a second opinion? Healing starts the day the tooth is removed, but it takes a while for gum to cover the socket where the tooth was, and for bone to fill in the hole underneath the gum. Often, an x-ray will show the outline of where the tooth was for years after the extraction because teeth are surrounded by a dense layer of bone.
