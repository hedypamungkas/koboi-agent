As Adults our total body water decreases to about 60%. By the age of 60 years the our total body water (TBW) decreases to about 50%. As we age... we dry out. Cerebro-Spinal Fluid (CSF) surrounds our brain and spinal cord. A mere 140 ml of fluid bathes the brain and fills the ventricles (internal cavities of the brain). This Cerebro-Spinal Fluid replenishes itself 3-4 times each day.
