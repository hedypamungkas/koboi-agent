SHRM is currently working on a certification program that will create a testing regime and governance model to provide integrity to the exam process. SHRM plans to offer the first exam for the new competency-based certification in mid-2015. However, to ensure that no applicants are disadvantaged by this transition, SHRM will continue to support the PHR and SPHR certifications programs through the December 2014 – January 2015 test window.
