Khufu's Great Pyramid. The Great Pyramid and its surrounding complex soon after its completion (Copyright Lee Krystek, 2010) It's 756 feet long on each side, 450 feet high and is composed of 2,300,000 blocks of stone, each averaging 2 1/2 tons in weight. Despite the makers' limited surveying tools, no side is more than 8 inches different in length than another, and the whole structure is perfectly oriented to the points of the compass.
