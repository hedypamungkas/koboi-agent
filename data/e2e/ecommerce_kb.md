# ShopWave — Customer Service Knowledge Base

## Returns & Refunds

### Return Policy
- **30-day return window** from delivery date for all items
- Items must be unused, in original packaging, with tags attached
- Electronics: 14-day return window (must include all accessories)
- Final sale items (marked "No Returns"): Not eligible for return
- Underwear and swimwear: Not eligible for return (hygiene reasons)

### Refund Processing
- Refund issued to **original payment method** within 5–7 business days
- Store credit: Instant (no waiting period)
- Gift returns: Issued as store credit to the gift recipient
- Partial returns (from a multi-item order): Pro-rated refund

### Return Shipping
- **Free return shipping** on orders over $50
- Orders under $50: $5.99 return shipping fee deducted from refund
- Drop-off at any ShopWave partner location: Free (find locations at shopwave.com/drops)

## Shipping

### Delivery Options
- **Standard**: 5–7 business days. Free on orders $35+
- **Express**: 2–3 business days. $9.99
- **Overnight**: Next business day (order by 2 PM EST). $19.99
- **International**: 10–21 business days. Calculated at checkout (duties not included)

### Order Tracking
- Tracking number emailed within 24 hours of shipment
- Track at shopwave.com/track or via the mobile app
- Delivery exceptions: Customer service will contact you within 48 hours

### Shipping Restrictions
- PO boxes: Standard shipping only (no Express/Overnight)
- APO/FPO: Standard only, 10–15 business days
- International: Some items restricted by destination country (check product page)

## Payment

### Accepted Methods
- Credit/Debit: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Digital: Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, ShopWave Wallet
- Buy Now Pay Later: Affirm (4 interest-free payments), Klarna
- Gift cards: Digital and physical (combine up to 5 per order)

### Payment Security
- All transactions encrypted with 256-bit SSL
- PCI DSS Level 1 compliant
- Fraud detection: Orders may be held for verification (1–2 business days)
- 3D Secure (Verified by Visa, Mastercard SecureCode) supported

## Product Warranty

### Electronics
- Manufacturer warranty: 1 year (standard), 2 years (premium brands)
- ShopWave extended warranty: Available at checkout ($15–$50 depending on item)
- Warranty claims: Contact manufacturer first, then ShopWave if unresolved

### Apparel & Home
- Quality guarantee: 60 days from purchase (tears, defects, color fading)
- Damage during shipping: Report within 48 hours of delivery for replacement

## Common Issues

### Missing Package
1. Check tracking status
2. Wait 24 hours past estimated delivery (carriers sometimes mark delivered early)
3. Check with neighbors/building management
4. Contact ShopWave for investigation (5–7 business day resolution)

### Damaged Item
1. Take photos of item and packaging
2. Report within 48 hours of delivery
3. Choose: replacement (free), refund, or store credit + 10% bonus
4. Keep original item if under $20 (no need to return)

### Wrong Item Received
1. Report within 30 days
2. Prepaid return label sent immediately
3. Correct item shipped upon return scan (don't wait for processing)
4. $10 store credit issued as apology
