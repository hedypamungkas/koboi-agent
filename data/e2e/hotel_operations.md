# Grand Plaza Hotel — Operations Manual

## Room Types & Rates (per night, USD)

### Standard Room
- **Rate**: $120/night
- **Size**: 25 sqm
- **Bed**: 1 Queen or 2 Twin
- **Amenities**: WiFi, TV, AC, mini-fridge, coffee maker
- **Capacity**: 2 adults + 1 child

### Deluxe Room
- **Rate**: $180/night
- **Size**: 35 sqm
- **Bed**: 1 King
- **Amenities**: All Standard + bathtub, bathrobe, city view, espresso machine
- **Capacity**: 2 adults + 2 children

### Executive Suite
- **Rate**: $320/night
- **Size**: 55 sqm
- **Bed**: 1 King + living area
- **Amenities**: All Deluxe + separate lounge, work desk, Nespresso, premium toiletries, access to Executive Lounge (free breakfast + evening cocktails)
- **Capacity**: 3 adults + 2 children

### Presidential Suite
- **Rate**: $850/night
- **Size**: 120 sqm
- **Bed**: 1 King + 2 Twin (convertible)
- **Amenities**: All Executive + private butler, jacuzzi, dining room, piano, terrace with ocean view
- **Capacity**: 4 adults + 2 children
- **Minimum stay**: 2 nights

## Hotel Policies

### Check-in / Check-out
- Check-in: 3:00 PM
- Check-out: 11:00 AM
- Early check-in: Free if room available (request at front desk)
- Late check-out: $30 until 2:00 PM, $60 until 6:00 PM (subject to availability)

### Cancellation Policy
- Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in
- Within 48 hours: 1 night charge
- No-show: Full booking amount charged
- Non-refundable rates: No cancellation allowed (clearly marked at booking)

### Pet Policy
- Small pets (under 15 kg) welcome in Standard and Deluxe rooms only
- Pet fee: $25/night
- Maximum 2 pets per room
- Service animals exempt from fees and size restrictions
- Pets not allowed in restaurant, pool, or spa areas

### Parking
- Self-parking: $15/night
- Valet parking: $25/night
- Electric vehicle charging: Free for hotel guests (2 stations available)

## Amenities

### Dining
- **The Terrace Restaurant**: Breakfast buffet ($25), lunch, dinner. Open 6:30 AM – 10:30 PM
- **Rooftop Bar**: Cocktails and light bites. Open 5:00 PM – 1:00 AM. 21+ only
- **Room Service**: 24 hours. $5 delivery fee. Full menu available

### Recreation
- Outdoor pool: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM. Heated year-round
- Fitness center: 24 hours. Key card access required
- Spa: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM. Reservations recommended. Treatments from $80
- Business center: 24 hours. Free printing (up to 20 pages)

### Services
- Concierge: 24 hours. Tours, tickets, restaurant reservations
- Laundry: Same-day if submitted before 10:00 AM. Express service +50%
- Airport shuttle: Free. Every 30 minutes 5:00 AM – 11:00 PM. Reservation required
- Babysitting: Available. $20/hour. 24-hour advance booking required

## Loyalty Program — Plaza Rewards
- Silver (10+ stays): 10% off rack rate, late checkout free
- Gold (25+ stays): 15% off, room upgrades, lounge access, free breakfast
- Platinum (50+ stays): 20% off, guaranteed suite upgrade, complimentary spa credit ($50)

## Group Bookings
- 10+ rooms: Contact groups@grandplaza.com for negotiated rates
- Conference rooms: $200–$800/day depending on size (4 rooms available, 10–200 capacity)
- Wedding package: From $5,000. Includes venue, catering for 50 guests, decoration, coordinator
