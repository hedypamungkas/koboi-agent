# Acme Corp Company Policy

## 1. Working Hours
- Monday - Friday: 08:00 - 17:00
- Lunch break: 12:00 - 13:00
- Saturday - Sunday: Off

## 2. Annual Leave
- Permanent employees: 12 days per year
- Contract employees: 6 days per year
- Unused leave can be carried forward maximum 3 days to the following year

## 3. Remote Work Policy
- Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week
- Prior approval from direct supervisor required
- Must be available during core hours (10:00 - 15:00)
- Company equipment must be used for work purposes only

## 4. Allowances
- Meal allowance: $150/month
- Transportation allowance: $100/month
- Health insurance: Coverage for employee and immediate family
- Internet allowance: $50/month for remote work days

## 5. Code of Conduct
- Maintain confidentiality of company data
- No gifts from vendors/clients permitted
- Professional attire required in office
- Maintain clean workspace environment
- Respect colleagues and foster inclusive culture
- Report any ethical concerns to HR or compliance hotline

## 6. Reporting System
- Daily reports: Submit to supervisor before 17:00
- Weekly reports: Due every Friday
- Monthly reports: Submit before the 5th of the following month

## 7. Office Facilities
- WiFi: 100 Mbps speed, for work purposes only
- Parking: Free for motorcycles, $50/month for cars
- Prayer room: Located on 2nd floor
- Cafeteria: Open 07:00 - 18:00
