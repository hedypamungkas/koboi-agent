# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## Welcome
Welcome to Acme Corp! We're thrilled to have you join our team. We hope you have a fulfilling career journey with us.

## Vision and Mission
- **Vision**: To be the leading technology company delivering innovative solutions
- **Mission**: To provide innovative, accessible, and affordable technology solutions that empower businesses worldwide

1. **Engineering**: Software and hardware product development

4. **HR**: Human resource management
5. **Finance**: Financial management and accounting
6. **Support**: Customer technical support

## Onboarding Process
### Week 1
- Day 1: Office orientation and team introductions
- Days 2-3: Product training and internal tools training
- Days 4-5: Begin assigned tasks with mentorship

### Month 1
- Start assigned responsibilities with mentoring support
- Complete mandatory compliance training
- Set goals with manager

### Month 3
- Probation review and performance evaluation
- Confirmation of employment status
- Career development planning

## Employee Benefits
- Health insurance (employee + immediate family)
- 401(k) retirement plan with company match
- Annual performance bonus
- Training and certification sponsorship
- Flexible working hours (core hours: 10:00-15:00)
- Remote work: 2 days per week
- Paid time off: 12 days annually
- Parental leave: 12 weeks
- Employee assistance program

## Career Levels
- Level 1: Junior (0-2 years experience)
- Level 2: Mid-level (2-5 years experience)
- Level 3: Senior (5-8 years experience)
- Level 4: Lead (8+ years experience)
- Level 5: Manager (as needed)

## Performance Review
- Quarterly check-ins with manager
- Annual performance review
- 360-degree feedback for senior roles
- Performance-linked compensation adjustments

## Resignation Procedure
- Notice period: 30 days
- Document and access handover
- Exit interview with HR
- Company asset return
- Final settlement processing

## Core Values
- **Innovation**: Embrace creativity and new ideas
- **Integrity**: Act with honesty and transparency
- **Excellence**: Deliver quality in everything we do
- **Collaboration**: Work together to achieve more
- **Customer Focus**: Put customers first in all decisions

## Equal Opportunity
Acme Corp is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
